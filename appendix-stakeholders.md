# Appendix: Participant Information

*This document provides anonymized information about the 18 researchers whose statements were analyzed for this study. All participants were drawn from a broader study of 25 domain experts across five stakeholder groups.*

## Participant Selection

### Selection Criteria
Participants were identified as actively engaged in research based on:
- Current involvement in scholarly projects using digital archives
- Regular use of archival platforms for research purposes

Roles ranged from historians and archivists to participants with more technical or infrastructural orientations, though all had direct, sustained engagement with primary sources.
